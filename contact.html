To contact Google Chrome, you can:

Report an issue or send feedback

Open Chrome, click More in the top right, click Help, and then add details about the issue. You can also include a screenshot, email address, or web address.

Call Google customer care

Call (650) 253-0000 or (650) 661 0004, select your language, and then choose the IVR option.

Contact Google Experts

If you have a Google One plan, you can contact Google Experts for help through chat, phone call, or email.

For Chrome Enterprise and Education, you can get support if your organization has at least 100 Chrome Enterprise Upgrade licenses. You can also get support if you are a Google Workspace customer or have Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Enhanced or Premium support.
